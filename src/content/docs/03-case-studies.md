# Ground Truth Validation

The DRTA 2.5 engine's mathematical constants were rigorously calibrated against real-world historical data from iconic startups. We use a **"Trimmed Mean"** method over a 48-week simulation (removing the top 6 and bottom 6 weeks) to find the stable core conversion rate.

## 1. Slack (2014) - The B2B PLG Benchmark
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.90 (Browser-based, extremely easy to invite team members).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.75 (The infamous 10,000 message search limit).
- **Ground Truth**: ~30%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **28.87%** 🎯

## 2. Notion (2018) - The High-Friction Prosumer
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.30 (Blank canvas anxiety, steep learning curve).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.35 (Generous free tier for individuals).
- **Ground Truth**: 5% - 10%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **6.88%** 🎯

## 3. GitHub (2011) - The Developer Utility
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.90 (Easy to browse, sign up, and fork).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.30 (Public repos are free, pay only for private).
- **Ground Truth**: 3% - 5%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **5.08%** 🎯

## 4. Zoom (2013) - The Freemium Trap
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.95 (Click link to join, no account needed).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.30 (40-minute limit on group calls, 1-on-1 free forever).
- **Ground Truth**: 4% - 5%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **4.37%** 🎯

## 5. Discord (2015) - The Absolute Free Tier
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.99 (Completely frictionless).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.05 (100% core features free, only cosmetic Nitro upgrades).
- **Ground Truth**: ~1.5%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **1.29%** 🎯

## 6. Canva (2013) - The Micro-Transaction Trap
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.95 (Extremely smooth drag-and-drop web editor).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.20 (Only pay $1 for premium stock photos, otherwise entirely free).
- **Ground Truth**: 2% - 4%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **3.7%** 🎯
