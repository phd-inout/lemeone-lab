# The 14D Commercial DNA Model

Lemeone-Lab treats startups not as business plans, but as physical entities defined by a 14-dimensional vector. When this vector collides with an agent population in the sandbox, conversion and survival naturally emerge.

## Core Dimensions (D1-D4): The Value Engine
These dimensions dictate the raw **Resonance** (how much people want the product).
- **D1 (Performance)**: Speed, latency, basic reliability.
- **D2 (Depth)**: Feature completeness for power users.
- **D3 (Interaction)**: UX fluidity and aesthetic delight.
- **D4 (Stability)**: Security, uptime, and trust.

## The Funnel Gates (D5-D6): The Business Model
These are the most critical dimensions for modeling the difference between user growth and actual revenue. They were decoupled in DRTA v2.5 to simulate real-world funnels accurately.
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: Determines *Adoption Rate*. 1.0 means instant browser access without sign-up (e.g., Discord). 0.0 means enterprise sales calls or heavy setup (e.g., legacy CRM).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: Determines *Conversion Rate*. 1.0 means strict limits that force payment (e.g., Slack's 10k messages). 0.0 means completely optional/decorative payment (e.g., Discord Nitro).

## Market Dynamics (D7-D9)
- **D7 (Unique Value)**: Differentiation against the existing market baseline.
- **D8 (Social Virality)**: Inherent K-factor (e.g., watermarks, invite loops).
- **D9 (Consistency)**: Execution delivery matching marketing promises.

## Strategic Future (D10-D13)
- **D10 (Ecosystem)**: Platform effects, APIs, developer network.
- **D11 (Barriers)**: Moats against cloning (data gravity, network effects).
- **D12 (Global Appeal)**: Cross-cultural / cross-language viability.
- **D13 (Growth Curve)**: The shape of the expected scaling function.

## Go-To-Market (D14)
- **D14 (Awareness)**: The initial visibility of the product on Day 1. Driven by PR, founder reputation, or ad spend.

## 现实锚定系统 (Deep Research)
Lemeone-Lab 并非运行在随机的真空中。

在提取 DNA 的同时，我们的 **News Parser Service** 会抓取真实互联网的最新行业报告、新闻动态与竞对动作。这些实时数据会被转化为“宏观重力常数”和“扰动参数”注入沙盒。

这意味着：如果你正在模拟一个 AI 视频工具，而 OpenAI 刚刚发布了 Sora，沙盒中的市场重力会立即发生偏移，从而改变你产品的存活率。
